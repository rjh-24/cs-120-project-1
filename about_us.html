About Us

Welcome to Days of Yore, where nostagia meets electrifying performances! We are a cover band dedicated to bringing back timeless hits that defined generations with an infusion of modern techniques.
Our members include:

  • Ella James, whose powerful voice and captivating stage presence makes every song come alive
  • Mark Rivers, guitarist with sweet technique and intricate riffs
  • Sammy Keys on drums with rhythmic beats that keep the band forward and infectious energy

Together, we celebrate the music that shaped our lives, from classic rock anthems to soul-stirring ballads. Join us as we take you on a journey through the Days of Yore!
